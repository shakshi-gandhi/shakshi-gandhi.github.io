About Me

I am Shakshi Gandhi – a curious, detail-driven data professional with hands-on experience at ZS, Oracle, and Colgate. I’ve worked across analytics, data science, consulting, and business insights, and what’s always excited me most is figuring out the why behind the numbers.

Now, as a graduate student at the University of Washington, I’m looking to contribute to a team that values thoughtful problem-solving and uses data to tell meaningful stories.
