About Me

I am Shakshi Gandhi, a data professional with ~3 years' experience across ZS, Oracle, and Colgate.

I have worked across analytics, data science, consulting, and business insights, and what’s always excited me most is figuring out the why behind the numbers.

Across projects, I’ve found joy not just in building solutions, but in understanding why something didn’t work the first time. Whether it was optimizing $650MM in budget forecasts at ZS or rebuilding processes at Oracle, I have learned that real impact often comes from digging into the messy middle - asking questions, brainstorming, and not being afraid to start over if needed.

Now, as a graduate student at the University of Washington, I’m looking to contribute to a team that values thoughtful problem-solving and uses data to tell clear, meaningful stories. I would love to explore how I could bring that mindset to your team.

If there’s a spot on your team, I would appreciate the chance to share how I might be helpful.

When I’m not working, you can find me trying to cook something new.
